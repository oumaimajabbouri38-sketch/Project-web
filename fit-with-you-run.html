RUNNING

Running is one of the simplest and most effective forms of exercise for improving overall fitness and health. It helps strengthen the heart, increase stamina, and burn calories. Running also improves mental health by reducing stress and boosting mood through the release of endorphins. It does not require special equipment, only comfortable shoes and motivation to start. Whether you run slowly or at a fast pace, consistency is the key to achieving better results and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
