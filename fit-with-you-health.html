HEALTH

Health is very essential for a good and happy life. It means being physically, mentally, and socially well, not just free from disease. A healthy lifestyle includes eating balanced food, drinking enough water, and doing regular exercise like walking or running. Sleep is also essential because it helps the body recover and stay energetic.Mental health matters too. Staying positive, reducing stress, and spending time with family and friends improves emotional well-being. Avoiding unhealthy habits like smoking and junk food helps keep the body strong.In short, good health comes from simple daily habits and consistency.
